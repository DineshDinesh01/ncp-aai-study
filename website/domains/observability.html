Run, Monitor & Maintain (Observability)

Tracing execution graphs, monitoring for model drift, and ensuring safe autonomous operations in production.

Why This Domain Matters

Because agents operate autonomously by definition, tracking why they took certain actions (e.g. which tool they invoked and with what data) is critical for debugging and lifecycle management. The exam expects you to understand traces, spans, and observability frameworks like LangSmith and OpenTelemetry.

Topics in This Domain

📡

LangSmith & OpenTelemetry

Logging agent runs, viewing internal thoughts and tool usage, benchmarking performance, and integrating tracing into architectures.
